Welcome


This is the Faculty of Family Medicine, West African College of Physicians (WACP) App.

Its aim is to simplify and coordinate the activities of the faculty.

Update Course


The activities for our update courses are now coordinated on the app. Resource persons will find it easier to prepare their contents ahead of their lectures. Participants will be able to access the materials for their update course anytime from the app.


Examinations


The Faculty App simplifies the coordination of examinations conducted by the Faculty of Family Medicine, West African College of Physician. Candidates get a fairer examination process. Examiners are selected in a fair, transparent manner.
